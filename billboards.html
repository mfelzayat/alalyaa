Choosing a Direction

Considerations
  • Recognition at distance — Which mark reads first at 50m+?
  • Brand register — Does it feel like a developer, a foundation, or a museum?
  • Scalability — How does the logo perform on smaller assets (signage, stationery, digital)?
  • Bilingual balance — Arabic-English coherence in the lockup.
